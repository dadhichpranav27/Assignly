VOLUNTEER PAGE !

We’re thrilled to welcome you to our volunteer platform! Here, you can share your details, showcase your handwriting, and take part in meaningful writing tasks that help students complete their assignments on time. Beyond gaining experience, you also have the opportunity to earn through your work, with rewards based on the type and timeliness of each task.
Communication and task updates will be handled directly through WhatsApp to ensure smooth coordination and quick responses.

Payment & Delivery Details

Assignly provides clear and timely compensation for all volunteer roles. The table below summarises the pay structure and delivery allowance. Please read carefully to understand how earnings and delivery reimbursements are calculated.

Writing Volunteers receive 70% of the total assignment amount for completing handwriting tasks to the required standard, with an additional 5% bonus for early and high-quality submissions — reaching up to 75% in total.

Delivery Volunteers are paid a delivery allowance: ₹50 for trips within 5 km, and an additional ₹8 per km for each kilometre beyond 5 km.

All-Round Volunteers who handle both writing and delivery tasks receive 85% of the total assignment amount, with an additional 5% bonus for early completion and delivery — bringing their total potential payout up to 90%.

A punctuality/quality reward is available for volunteers who submit or deliver work properly and on time.

Volunteer Type Role Payment Bonus
Writing Volunteer Write assignments neatly & on time 70% of assignment value (+5%) for early & quality submission
Delivery Volunteer Collect & deliver assignments ₹50 (≤5 km) + ₹8/km beyond For proper & timely delivery
All-Round Volunteer Write & deliver assignments 85% of assignment value (+5%) for early writing & delivery

Payments are processed after verification of work. For delivery tasks, volunteers must confirm the delivery of the hardcopy. If you have a preferred payout method, include it in your profile above in the registration form.
